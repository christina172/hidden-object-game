Hidden Object Game

Find hidden objects by clicking on them and selecting their names from the dropdown list. If you are correct, a green border will appear around the object. You need to find 14 objects: a saxophone, a chinese fan, a butterfly, a bug, a key, a turtle, an hourglass, a hat, a magic lamp, a playing card, milk, scallions, cheese, a brooch.

Congratulations! You won.
